How to Vote

1. Register to Vote

Apply for a Voter ID (EPIC) via Form 6 on the NVSP portal or Voter Helpline App. You must be an Indian citizen and 18+ years old.

2. Check Voter List

Verify your name in the Electoral Roll online or via SMS to ensure you can vote.

3. Find Polling Booth

Locate your designated polling station online using your EPIC number.

4. Cast Your Vote

Bring your Voter ID (or approved alternative ID). Press the EVM button next to your chosen candidate, and verify via VVPAT.
